## Overview
In this assignment, you will be using a variety of different classification models on the (in)famous [Iris Dataset](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iris_flower_data_set). Essentially, the dataset records information on the recorded attributes of three species of flowers that look quite similar.

![Iris Setosa, Iris Versicolor, and Iris Viginica respectively](./flowers.jpeg)

You can read more details about the dataset in the [SciKit Learn documentation](https://scikit-learn.org/stable/datasets/toy_dataset.html#iris-dataset).
